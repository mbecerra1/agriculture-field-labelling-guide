Guidelines for Labelling Oil Palm Fields in the Amazon

A step-by-step approach for accurate mapping

1. Description

High-resolution satellite base maps allowed interpreters to interpretating different types of perennial agriculture. In this case, you'll have as resource base map: Bing maps, Google Satellite Imagery, Planet monthly mosaic, Sentinel 1 and Sentinel 2.     Types of Observation

    We will use at least three source of information to make the interpretation

    • A high resolution basemap (either Google Satellite Imagery or Bings Maps or MapBox satellite)
    • Planet NICFI mosaic, selecting imagery from June to December 2024
    • A sentinel-1, selecting imagery from June to December 2024
    • Geodash tool, selecting the EVI graph from 2018 to 2024 to understand the temporality of the field

    2. Identification Criteria: Oil Palm

    • Field Shape: Look for regular, and geometric rectangular shapes .
    • Vegetation Appearance: Cropland typically shows uniform vegetation cover, where it is a visible palm similar to examples below, surrounded by larger area of palms, darker green
    • Surrounding Context: Defined planting plots
